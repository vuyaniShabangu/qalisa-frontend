About Us

Our Story

Qalisa Hub (to start / to initiate) is focused on ‘building entrepreneurs ground-up with a focus on start-up and early-stage township entrepreneurs. According to … the township economy is estimated to contribute in excess of R100bn to the SA economy, and is seen as a major contributor to both GDP and employment. Small and medium businesses will account for up to 90% of new job creation by 2030 according to….

Qalisa Hub was founded by qualified and vastly experienced chartered accountants, start-up investors and entrepreneurs Hiten Keshave and Sanjay Soni, who have a desire to ‘pay forward’ their experiences and beliefs to the many emerging township entrepreneurs.

Qalisa Hub aims to bridge the gap between idea and commercial viability. Qalisa Hub was borne out of a need to provide a differentiated intervention to township entrepreneurs by providing them with much needed practical ‘hand-holding’ in the formative stages of their entrepreneurship journey.

Qalisa Hub has teamed up with a number of seasoned executives, entrepreneurs, subject matter advisors and business professionals to offer qualifying township entrepreneurs the opportunity to fast-track their businesses to become investor ready.

Over a relatively short 3–6-month period, each qualifying entrepreneur will have access to a panel of experienced advisors, who will work with them intensely to get their ideas investor-ready and lay the foundations for exponential business growth.

Mission statement

To connect township start-ups and entrepreneurs with experienced and established advisors from various business sectors, to help accelerate the development and commercialisation of business ideas and concepts.

Positioning statement

Qalisa works with township entrepreneurs to help accelerate business concepts and ideas into a commercially viable business that is investor ready.

Value proposition

Seasoned subject matter or (experts) advisors fast-tracking an entrepreneur’s ideas/concepts into a reality, to the point the idea becomes investor ready.
